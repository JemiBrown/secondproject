Attitude of Gratitude

How to use this attitude change

I believe an adttitude of gratitude is one of the most effective coping skills someone can have.

It is really easy to get going, you just start with the small things you have in your life. A bed to sleep in or gas in your car.



Starting a gratitude journal is another way you can practice expanding the things you are grateful for.

You can find ideas for journal prompts here.

Once I began thanking the universe for everything its given me I noticed that my attitude changed and it felt like weight lifted off of my chest. I make it a point to spend time alone at least once a week and name all the new things for the week that I have in my life.
